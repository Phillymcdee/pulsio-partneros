# Metrics & Acceptance Criteria

## Product KPIs
- **Precision@5** (top 5 relevance via thumbs): ≥60% by week 2.
- **Latency** (first insight after adding partner): ≤10 minutes.
- **Actions/week** (with drafts): ≥2/user for MVP.

## Outcome KPIs (pilot)
- **Reply rate:** ≥8% on sent outreach.
- **Meetings:** ≥1/month per active user (target).

## Acceptance criteria (MVP)
- Add partners, objectives, and channel → receive first digest with ≥3 items.
- Each item includes: Partner, Signal, Score, Why, Action, Outreach draft, Evidence link.
- Thumbs up/down and Mark N/A persist & influence future rankings.
- Score breakdown visible (objective fit vs signal strength).
